# Academy Content Model

Academy content is conceptually separated into four groups:

- Learning Paths: assigned role sequences such as Operations and Executive Assistant - Trial Onboarding.
- Courses and Modules: sequenced teaching content with objectives, resources, checks, acknowledgement rules, and versioning.
- SOP Library: step-by-step recurring procedures with owner, trigger, inputs, approvals, escalation, and completion criteria.
- Knowledge Base: reference articles that may not require sequential completion.

Seed-managed content uses `sourceKey` and `seedManaged`. The seed updates records only while `seedManaged` is true.
